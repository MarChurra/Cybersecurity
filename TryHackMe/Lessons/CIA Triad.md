### CID Triad
- In cyber security, being secure means ensuring specific conditions for the digital data. These conditions are these key aspects:
  - Confidentiality
  - Integrity
  - Availability

### Confidentiality
- Ensures that sensitive data can only be accessed by auhthorized individuals, otherwise there can be financial loss, privacy violations or legal consequences.
- For this, processes such as encryption and access controls are employed.

### Integrity
- Ensures that authorized individuals do not modify data. Without integrity, data can be altered and no longer be trusted, that could lead to dangerous consequences.

### Availability
- Ensures that data and services are available to authorized users when needed.
