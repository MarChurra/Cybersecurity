## Transfer
- Move the risk to another party
- Buy some cybersecurity insurance
- ACcept
  - A business decision: We wil take the risk
  - Often the usual course
- Accept with exemption
  - A securtity policy or regulation cannot be followed
  - May be based on available security controls, size of the organization, total assets, etc.
  - Exemption may need approval
- Accept with exception
  - Internal security policies are not applied
  - Monthly security updates must be applied within 3 calendar days
  - The monthly updates causes a critical software package to crash
  - An expection is made to the update timeframe
 - Avoid
   - Stolp participaing in a high-risk activity
   - Removes the risk
 - Mitigate
   - Decrease the rism level
   - Invest in security systems

  ## Risk Reporting
  - A formal document
    - Identifies risk
    - Detailed information for each risk
  - Usually created for senior management
    - Makes decisions regarding resources, budgeting, additional security tasks
  - Commonly includes critical and emerging risks
