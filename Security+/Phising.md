## Phising
- Social engineering with a touch of spoofing
  - Often delivered by email, text, etc
  - Very remarkable when well done
- Check the URL and the sender address
- Usually something not quite right
  - Spelling, fonts, graphisc

## business email compromise
- We trust email sources
  - The attacker takes advantage of this trust
  - Not really a legitimate email address
- Financial Fraud
  - Sends email with updated bank information
  - Modify wire transfer details
- The recipient clicks the links
  - The attachments have malware
 
## Tricks and misdirection
- How are they so sucessful
  - Digital slight of hand
  - It fools the best of us
- Typosquatting
  - A type of URL hijacking
- Pretexting
  - Lying to get information
  - Attacker is a character in a situation they create
  - Hi, we´re calling from VISA regarding an automated payment to your utility service.

   ## Phising with different bait
  - Vishing (Voice Phising) over the phone or voicemail
    - Caller ID spoofing is common
    - Fake security checks
  - Smishing (SMS phising) is done via text message.
    - Spoofing is a problem here as well
    - Forward links or asks for personal information
  - Variations on a theme
    - The fake check scam, phone verification code scam, BOSS CEO Scam, advanced-free scam.
