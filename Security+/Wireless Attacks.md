## 802.11 management frames
- 802.11 wireless includes a number of managament features
  - Frames that make everything work
- Important for the operation of 802.11 wireless
  - How to find AP; manage QoS, associate/dissasociate with an AP
  - Original Wireless standard dit not add protection for management frames

## Protecting against deauth attacks
- IEE has already addressed the problem
  - Updates included with 802.11ac
- Some of the important management frames are encrypted
  - Dissaociate, deauthenticate, channel switch announcements, etc.
- Not everything is encrypted
  - Beacons, probes, authentication, association
