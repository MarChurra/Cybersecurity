## Tabletop Exercises
- Performing a full-scale disaster drill can be costly and time consuming
- Many of the logistics can be determined through analysis, without goin g through a disaster drill
- Get key players together for a tabletop exercise
  - Talk through a simulated disaster

## Fail Over
- Check is the FailOver happens automatically
  - Plan for the Worst
- Create a redundant infrastructure
  - Multiple routers, firewalls, switches, etc.
- If one stops working, fail over to the operational unit
  - Many infrastructure devices and servies can do this automatically.

## Simulation
- Test with a simulated event
  - Phising attack, passwords requests, data breaches
- Going phising

  - Send to your actual user community
  - See who bites
- Test Internal Security
  - Did the phising get past the filer?
- Test the users
  - Who clicked?
  - ADditional training may be required

## Parellel processing
- Split a process through multiple parallel CPUs
  - A single computer with multipe CPU cores or multiply physical CPUs and Multiple computers
- Improves performance and recovery
  - Split complex transactions across multiple processors
  - Quickly identify a faulty system
  - Take the faulty device out of the list of available processors
  - Continue operatin gthe remaining processors
