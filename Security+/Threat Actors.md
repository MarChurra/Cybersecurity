## Unskilled Attackers
- Runs pre-made scripts without any knowledge of what is really happening
  - Anyone can do this
- Motivated by the hunt
  - Disrurption, data exfiltration, sometimes philosophical or political
- Can be internal or external, but usually external
- Not very sophisticated
- No formal funding

## Hacktivist
- A hacker with a purpose
- Often an external entity
- Can be remarkably sophisticated
- Funding may be limited

## Insider Threat
- More than just passwords on sticky notes
  - Motivated by revenge, financial gain
- Extensive Resources
  - Using th eorganization´s resources against themselves
- An internal entity
  - Eating away from the inside
- Medium level of sophistication
  - The insider has institutional knowledge
  - Attacks can be directed at vulnerable systems
  - The insider knows what to hit
